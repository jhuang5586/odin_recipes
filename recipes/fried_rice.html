Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a great way to use up your left over rice.

Ingredients

  • left over rice
  • 2 eggs
  • Sausages
  • scallions

Steps

  1. put oil in a wok and cook the eggs
  2. throw in the sausages an continue to cook
  3. throw in the rice and keep cooking
  4. when everything is cooked, add in the chopped scallions and serve!
